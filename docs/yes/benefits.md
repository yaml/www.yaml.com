## How to Choose

### Choose YES Builder if:
- You are an early-stage startup or small team
- You want to support YAML open source sustainability
- You need basic early security alerts and standard support

### Choose YES Member if:
- YAML is important but not mission-critical in your stack
- You need occasional expert support and guidance
- You want to contribute to YAML sustainability while gaining value
- Budget is a primary consideration

### Choose YES Partner if:
- YAML is critical to your product or stack
- You need regular strategic guidance and support
- You want deeper collaboration with YAML leadership
- You need comprehensive security coverage

### Choose YES Leader if:
- YAML is mission-critical, business-essential in your stack
- You require premium support with guaranteed response
- You want maximum influence over what YAML builds next
- You need a true partnership relationship
