## The YES Program

Our primary offering is the **YES (YAML Enterprise Sustainability)** program—enterprise support from the people who built YAML.

**Every YES member gets:**

- 48-hour early security notifications
- Professional support from YAML experts
- Strategic influence to help decide what YAML builds next
- Access to member forum and town halls

## Professional Services

Available to YES members and as standalone engagements:

- **Security** - Audits, vulnerability assessments, and remediation planning
- **Training** - YAML fundamentals, advanced topics, and YAMLScript workshops
- **Consulting** - Architecture review, migration planning, performance optimization
- **Custom Development** - Sponsor features, library extensions, integration work

YES members receive preferred pricing on all services.
