# Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 12, 2026

The YAML Company ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your personal data and respecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

## 1. Data Controller
The YAML Company is the data controller for the processing of your personal data collected through this website. If you have any questions, you can contact us at [privacy@yaml.com](mailto:privacy@yaml.com).

## 2. Personal Data We Collect
We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you register for early access or contact us:
* **Name**
* **Corporate Email Address**
* **Organization/Company**
* **Role/Job Title**
* **Critical dependencies / libraries of interest** (via the Dependency Manifest Survey)

## 3. How We Use Your Data
We process your personal data for the following purposes:
* **Early Access Verification:** To verify your application and eligibility for early access to YES Program binaries.
* **Service Announcements:** To notify you as soon as the binaries and compliance assets are ready for your organization.
* **Updates and News:** If you explicitly opt-in, we will send you updates and news regarding YAML Enterprise Sustainability and related developments.
* **Website Analytics:** We analyze site usage using Google Analytics to optimize performance and content.

## 4. Legal Basis for Processing
* **Consent:** We process your marketing subscription based on your explicit opt-in consent.
* **Legitimate Interest:** We process your application data to prepare and evaluate early access qualifications, and to communicate service readiness.
* **Contractual Preparation:** To align on service agreements and procurement conditions for the YES Program.

## 5. Third-Party Service Providers
We share form submission data with:
* **Google Forms & Sheets:** For secure processing and storage of applications.
* **Google Analytics:** For anonymized site usage analysis.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to any other third parties.

## 6. Retention and Security
We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected. We implement standard security measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, loss, or alteration.

## 7. Your GDPR Rights
Under the GDPR, you have the following rights:
* **Right of Access:** You can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
* **Right to Rectification:** You can ask us to correct incorrect or incomplete data.
* **Right to Erasure (Right to be Forgotten):** You can request that we delete your personal data.
* **Right to Withdraw Consent:** You can opt-out of marketing communications at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, please email us at [privacy@yaml.com](mailto:privacy@yaml.com).
